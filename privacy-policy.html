Privacy Policy

FinTrack — Personal Finance, Completely Private

Effective: February 8, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 18, 2026

🔒 Our Core Privacy Principle

FinTrack is a local-first, privacy-first application. All your financial data stays on your device. We operate no servers, collect no personal information, and transmit nothing.

🚫 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your financial transactions, budgets, or savings goals
  • Your location or device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Any data via third-party analytics (Firebase, Amplitude, Mixpanel, etc.)

📱 Data Stored on Your Device

All App data lives locally on your device:

  • Financial data (transactions, budgets, savings goals, categories) — on-device encrypted database
  • App preferences (theme, currency, notifications) — device local storage
  • Security credentials (PIN hash) — device secure storage (Keychain / EncryptedSharedPreferences)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the Backup & Export feature.

🛡️ Encryption & Security

  • Financial database encrypted with AES-256
  • PIN stored as a cryptographic hash — never plain text
  • Biometric authentication handled entirely by your device OS — never accessible to the App

🔗 Third-Party Services

FinTrack does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services. The App has no network permissions and makes no outbound connections.

👤 Your Rights & Data Control

Because all data is stored locally, you have complete control:

  • Delete all data: Use "Clear Data" in Settings, or uninstall the App
  • Export your data: Use the Backup & Export feature in Settings
  • No account to delete: There are no server-side profiles associated with your use

👶 Children's Privacy

FinTrack does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App contains no features that target or collect data from minors.

📋 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects any revisions. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
