Privacy First

We can't see your data.
And that's by design.

FinTrack is built on a simple principle: your financial data belongs to you alone. We have no backend, no accounts, and no way to access your information — because we never receive it in the first place.

  • No name, email, or personal identifiers collected
  • No analytics or behavioural tracking
  • No third-party SDKs (Firebase, Amplitude, etc.)
  • No network permissions — the app has no internet access
  • All financial data encrypted with AES-256
  • PIN stored as a cryptographic hash, never plain text
